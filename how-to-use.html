How to Use These Books

This series is designed to prioritise accessibility, predictability, and the gradual development of skills, supporting students on their reading journey while minimising cognitive load.

Reading Approach

The texts can be read like other books, with flexibility to explore them in any order or across different sets. Students are encouraged to read with fluency and rhythm, developing natural and confident reading style. Each text is intentionally kept short to help students establish good reading habits.

Reinforcing Learning

Even if students memorise the content, instructors can strengthen learning by revisiting individual words. Isolating words and asking questions like, “What was this word?” supports long-term memory retention and reinforces vocabulary acquisition.

This series is thoughtfully crafted to inspire confidence and nurture growth in young readers.
